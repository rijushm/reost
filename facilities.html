Multi cuisine restaurant

Multi-cuisine restaurant serves savory delights for patrons from a variety of Indian and international menus. The aroma wafting from our kitchens is bound to set alight your appetite. while our deft staff never leaves you wanting for anything. The restaurant also fulfills room service orders.

Travel Desk

Green County aim to make your travel as hassle-free and seamless as possible while you’re with us. You can plan your stay with the help of our experienced team who is familiar with distances of all the tourist attractions and activities which it has to offer.

Lake View

Joyous boat rides – Paddle boats, Rowing boats are available for sporting and joy riding. Beautiful Shikaras with skilled boatmen are also available for sunset joy riding.

Activities

There are several activities at our resort e.g. Cricket, table tennis, volleyball, Carrom, Chess, usage of swimming pool & fitness center on availability. We have kids play area in all our hotels / resort especially for our youngest guests may spend enjoyful.

Fishing Area

Fishing yet continues to be a favourite pastime for Bengalis. Keeping that in mind, Malancha Nature Park provides wide opportunity for fishing lovers. Special pockets near the lake are reserved for fishing. Special boats are also available for fishing in the deeper areas.

Fun Filled & Picnic Zones

Friends, family, colleagues and a place to break free…”PICNIC” is the magic word… self-supporting womenfolk of the village provide suited assistance to get you the ingredients and basic amenities in place and make your picnic a grand success.
